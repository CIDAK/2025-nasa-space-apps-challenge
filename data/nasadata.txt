Other

MERIS (Envisat) – Primarily measures ocean color / chlorophyll, not temperature.
PACE-OCI (upcoming NASA mission) – Focused on ocean color and water quality.
S3A-OLCI & S3B-OLCI (Copernicus Sentinel-3) – Focused on ocean color, but Sentinel-3 also carries SLSTR for SST (so strictly, OLCI is more ocean color than temp).
SeaWiFS – Ocean color / chlorophyll monitoring, not temperature.
